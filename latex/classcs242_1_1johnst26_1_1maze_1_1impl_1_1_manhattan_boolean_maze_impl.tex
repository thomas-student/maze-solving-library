\subsection{Detailed Description}
An implementation of the \hyperlink{interfacecs242_1_1johnst26_1_1maze_1_1_maze}{Maze} Interface. This is a Manhattan-\/\-Boolean implementation, in that the spaces of the maze are stored as an 2\-D array of booleans containing whether or `not the space is valid or invalid (whether it is a wall or not). The Manhattan component of the name signifying that the distance between points is based on the Manhattan distance formula.
